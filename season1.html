Jujutsu Kaisen Season 1 introduces Yuji Itadori, a high schooler who becomes the vessel for the legendary curse, Ryomen Sukuna. Joining Tokyo Jujutsu High, he fights supernatural Curses alongside Megumi and Nobara. Under Satoru Gojo’s guidance, they battle deadly spirits, culminating in the intense, action-packed Kyoto Goodwill Event arc.
